Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Who is this bot for?
    Small to mid-sized Greenville RP servers who need basic session control tools.

  • 2. What does Plan A include?
    Only the bot source code, with no setup help or hosting.

  • 3. What does Plan B include?
    Source code, basic support, and bot hosting by CoreBots.

  • 5. What support do you offer?
    Plan B includes minor help and bug support. Plan A has no support.

  • 6. Can I upgrade later?
    Yes, Plan A users can later upgrade to Plan B through our Discord.

  • 7. How do I host the bot myself?
    You’ll receive a zip of the bot files and can host it anywhere (Replit, Cybrancee, etc).

  • 8. Do you update the bot?
    Only Plan B gets occasional updates or command changes if needed.

  • 9. Where can I order?
    All orders go through our Discord: Join here.
