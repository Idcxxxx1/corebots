Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Who is this bot for?
    Small to mid-sized Greenville RP servers who need basic session control tools.

  • 2. What does Plan A include?
    Only the bot source code, with no setup, no support, no hosting and no customization.

  • 3. What does Plan B include?
    A custom hosted bot based on the code of Plan A and support.

  • 5. What support do you offer?
    Plan B includes support for the hosted bot. Plan A has no support.

  • 6. Do you update the bot?
    No the code for both plans is as is, exceptions can be made on rare occasions.

  • 7. Where can I order?
    Head over to the order tab in the navigation bar and you will find all info there.

  • 8. How long does delivery take?
    Plan A will be delivered within 24 hours, for Plan B we'll start configuring your bot within 24h-48h.
